Ridgeway Country Club is a private golf club featuring one of the most challenging par-72 courses in the area. Offer the very best value in a private golf club while providing a destination, a home away from home where you can have a great time with good friends through golf and social events.

Fun for the whole family!

Ridgeway Country Club will host the Junior Golf Academy on Tuesdays, June 12th through August 7th. The Junior Club Championship and Junior Golf Banquet will be on August 9th. The juniors will learn all facets of the game including rules of golf, etiquette, short game, full-swing, bunker play, and course management. The Ridgeway Professional Golf Staff will run the Academy.

Our events

For an intimate wedding, family gathering, or corporate event, Ridgeway Country Club will provide your guests with a memorable experience. Our Clubhouse venue is complete with beautiful banquet spaces, full bar, lounge area, and an outside patio deck overlooking the stunning golf course.
